This is DatroNoise

I’m a composer, producer and multi-instrumentalist creating music across R&B, pop dance and trap.

Since 2019, I’ve been developing my sound independently through self-learning and a hands-on approach to composition and production. Along the way, I’ve expanded into electric guitar, classical guitar, piano and other instruments as different ways of exploring ideas.

My music is shaped by atmosphere, memory and feeling - often beginning with a visual moment, a sentence, or a sound I hear in everyday life before becoming music.

I’m not interested in making background music or staying in one emotion. I want to create records that feel immersive and open-ended - balancing warmth, nostalgia and space in a way that stays with you after the music ends.
